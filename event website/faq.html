Frequently Asked Questions!
What is Dramatica?
Each hour will be based on popular icons and divas (Gaga, Ariana, Miley, Beyonce, etc) where individuals will go head to head in their respective drag outfits and perform (lip sync) with a backing track. There will be a panel of judges present (similar to 90â€™s ballroom culture) to navigate the competition and pick finalists. The top 2 contestants with the highest scores will perform an ultimate freestyle showdown.

How can I register?
When viewing the set list page there is a down arrow located next to each artist and their respective time slot. Click the down arrow to view a handful of songs that can be performed at Dramatica! You have the option to listen to a 30 second preview and can even favorite select songs. Remember each guest can only perform once unless you are a finalist! The top 2 contestants with the highest scores will perform an ultimate freestyle showdown.
What's the purpose?
To create an event full of celebration and acceptance, post COVID-19, where allies and LGBTQ+ members can have a night of rejoice over the collective love of pop music, divas, and expression of drag.
Is this one-time only?
Currently, yes. But the team behind Dramatica is working as hard as possible to bring a joint run of events over summer 2021! Stay tuned!
Any performance limitations?
No nudity, acts of hatred or violence, no blood, gore, no performance length past 2 minutes and 30 seconds, no racism, no discrimination, no anti-semitism, etc. Dramatica exists to uplift and celebrate our diverse community! Any attempts to disrupt this peace will immediately be escorted off property.
